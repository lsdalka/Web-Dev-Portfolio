Community Center

A Wordpress website for Tchances, Community Center in Tottenham.
Developed to function as an one-page website, editing lightly the official Twenty Seventeen theme.
I went with a minimalist style, based on black and white in order to highlight better the colorful paintings.

The artwork was broken down into five categories and the photoswipe plugin was used for the thumbnails of the paintings to be exhibited with a masonry effect and when clicked to show in a dark full screen mode.

Different sizes of each painting are used for maximum quality and page load speed.
When in full screen view, under the artwork shows the title, the tools used and the dimensions.
Each painting can be shared at various social media with the click of a button or downloaded for personal use.
Biographical information and Contact details were added in their own pages.
The website is fully responsive and mobile friendly, quick to load even on weak connections and SEO optimized.


This website is a remake of the first website I have ever made (in 2014) and which got me into web development in the first place.
